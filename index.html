Frequently Asked Questions

How much does your service cost?

+

Our services start from as low as £2 per application. We believe in making UK career opportunities accessible to everyone with our affordable pricing structure.

What is the typical HCA starting salary?

+

NHS Healthcare Assistants typically start with a salary between £28,011 - £30,230, with opportunities for progression and overtime.

What are the basic requirements for NHS healthcare assistant roles?

+

Typically, you need a good level of English (IELTS or equivalent), basic literacy and numeracy skills, and a genuine desire to care for others. Previous healthcare experience is beneficial but not always required.

How long does the visa sponsorship process take?

+

The entire process from application to visa approval typically takes 3-6 months, depending on the NHS Trust and your individual circumstances.

Do I need to pay for the visa sponsorship?

+

The NHS Trust typically covers the cost of the Certificate of Sponsorship. However, you are responsible for visa application fees, Immigration Health Surcharge, and other associated costs.

Can my family join me in the UK?

+

Yes, healthcare assistants on a Health and Care Worker visa can bring dependents (spouse/partner and children under 18) to the UK.
